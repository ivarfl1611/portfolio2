Software I use, gadgets I love, and other things I recommend.

Welcome to my digital domain! I'm passionate about sharing the tools and gadgets that fuel my productivity and creativity. From Visual Studio Code for development and Figma for design to Notion for organization, I've curated a suite of software that's fast, collaborative, and highly customizable. My workstation, boasting a 16” MacBook Pro with M1 Max, Dell UltraSharp monitors, Keychron K2 keyboard, Logitech MX Master 3 mouse, Audio-Technica ATH-M50x headphones, and Rain Design mStand, is tailored for comfort and efficiency. With Alfred enhancing my productivity, join me on this journey of exploration at the intersection of technology and creativity.

Workstation

16” MacBook Pro, M1 Max, 64GB RAM (2022)

I was using an Intel-based 16” MacBook Pro prior to this and the difference is night and day. I’ve never heard the fans turn on a single time, even under the incredibly heavy loads I put it through with our various launch simulations.

Dell UltraSharp U2717D

I have two of these monitors and they’re great. I’ve had them for years and they’ve been rock solid.

Keychron K2

I’ve been using this keyboard for a few years now and I love it. It’s wireless, has a great feel, and the battery lasts forever.

Logitech MX Master 3

I’ve been using this mouse for a few years now and it’s great. It’s wireless, has a great feel, and the battery lasts forever.

Audio-Technica ATH-M50x

I’ve been using these headphones for a few years now and they’re great. They’re comfortable, sound good, and are built like a tank.

Rain Design mStand

I’ve been using this stand for my MacBook Pro for a few years now and it’s great. It’s sturdy, looks good, and keeps my laptop cool.
